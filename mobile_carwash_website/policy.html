Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Where do you operate?

We currently operate in Githurai, Ruiru, Juja, Thika, and Kiambu with both station-based and mobile wash options.

2. Do I need to provide water or electricity?

No, our mobile teams carry everything needed for the service, including water tanks and power supplies.

3. Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel up to 2 hours before your scheduled time without penalties. Late cancellations may incur charges.

4. What happens if it rains?

If weather conditions affect service delivery, we will reschedule at no extra cost. We will notify you in advance where possible.

5. How long does the wash take?

A full wash typically takes between 30 minutes to 1 hour depending on the package and vehicle condition.
